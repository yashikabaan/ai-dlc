# Meetings and Reporting

Last Updated: `<YYYY-MM-DD>`

## Daily Meeting Support
- Inputs: yesterday/today/blockers from each contributor.
- Output: shared daily summary with owners and due dates.
- Rule: unresolved blockers must have an explicit escalation owner.

## Daily Status Updates
- Format: use `docs/templates/daily-update-template.md`.
- Cadence: `<business day cadence>`.
- Audience: team + coordinator + interested stakeholders.

## Blocker Reporting
- Log blocker with:
  - blocker description
  - impact
  - owner
  - required decision/support
  - target resolution date
- Escalate if unresolved beyond `<threshold>`.

## Weekly Progress Checks
- Compare plan vs actual.
- Highlight completed outcomes, carry-over work, and risk trends.
- Use `docs/templates/weekly-summary-template.md`.

## Review Summaries
- For each sprint/release review, capture:
  - committed vs delivered scope
  - acceptance outcomes
  - notable quality or risk findings

## Retrospective Inputs
- Collect what helped, what hurt, what to improve.
- Track improvement actions with owner and due date.
- Revisit action status at next retrospective.
